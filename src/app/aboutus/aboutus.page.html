aboutus ABOUT US DONATE2NATION APP is a online platform that was created for those in need and want to donate. You can donate anything you want such as foods, toys, clothes and so on and we will deliver it for you to your respective donation place. AIMS Help and support those who are underprivileged and have difficulties in providing themselves and/or family with daily essentials. With the help from the donors, the receivers will feel included in the society, and help to reduce their burden. OBJECTIVE Raise awareness that even though majority Bruneians are living with privileges, there are actually a minority (roughly 5% of the population) of Bruneians that are living in poverty and need support from the public.
